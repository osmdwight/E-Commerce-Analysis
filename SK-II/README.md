## Insights Summary
---
In order to evaluate and reach the goal, I focused on the following key metrics:

* **Revenue**: The Monthly comparison of the overall generated revenue
* **Cart to Checkout Conversion Rate**: The percentage of users who added items to their cart and completed a purchase.
* **Viewer to Buyer Conversion Rate**: The percentage of users who viewed a store page or product and completed a purchase.
* **Redeemed Revenue**: Revenue generated from vouchers
* **ROI**: A multiplier that shows how much return is generated from discounted items relative to their cost.
* **Discount Cost**: The amount saved by the customer using the voucher.
* **Collected and Reedemed Rate**: The percentage of people who claimed the voucher and redeemed or used the voucher that was collected

**Revenue**
  * The monthly average revenue  was RM953K, ranging from RM311k to RM2.15M. November got the highest generated revenue (RM2.15M) while January got the lowest revenue with RM311K.
  * While November had the highest revenue, March had the highest increase of revenue with 254.57% vs previous month
  * March and November are in peaks due to Lazada Special Events such as Epic Birthday Sales in Lazada Malaysia and 11.11 promo

**Cart to Checkout Conversion Rate**
  * Across the month, August got the lowest conversion rate record with only 25.98% of items being purchased after it was added to customers cart.
  * November had the highest units added to cart but was able to outperformed other months, caused by special event
  * Overall the average conversion rate was 32.18%  for all months

**Viewer to Buyer Conversion Rate**
   * Across the month, March got the highest conversion rate  record with 1.49% of visitors pruchased atleast one item.
   * Not surprisingly, the month of May and November got the two highest record of visitors. While, January had the lowest visitors
   * Overall the average conversion rate was 0.82% across all month.
     
**Redeemed Revenue and ROI**

  * Surprisingly the month of April had the highest ROI with a record of 19.14x
